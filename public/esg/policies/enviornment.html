Health, Safety & Environment (HSE) Policy

Objective

NeoLiv is committed to conducting its real estate development activities in a manner that ensures health, safety, and well-being of its employees, contractors, visitors, and all connected stakeholders.

This policy also reinforces our commitment to environmental stewardship by minimizing the environmental impact of our operations.

Definition

The NeoLiv Group shall collectively mean all the entities associated with NeoLiv Capital Advisory, including NeoLiv Real Estate, Inliv Real Estate Fund, all the asset SPVs, including SPVs that shall form part of NCA in future.

Applicability

This Policy has been approved and adopted by the Board of Directors and is applicable to the Manager and NeoLiv Group.

Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party stakeholders involved in the operations, including all real estate development projects, regional and corporate offices.

Health & Safety

Leaders at all levels, demonstrate their personal commitment to H&S to promote Principle of Prevention of unsafe situations by integrating requirements from design & engineering stage to operation and maintenance

Environment

We recognize our responsibility to minimize environmental impact during the entire lifecycle of our projects. Therefore, we will:

  • Comply with all applicable national, regional, and local environmental laws, regulations and standards and endeavor to go beyond compliance.
  • Implement sustainable construction practices that focus on energy eÔ¨Éciency, waste reduction, water conservation and minimizing carbon footprints.
  • Continually improve the environmental performance by setting goals, targets, and processes for efficient use of natural resources, waste minimization, emission reduction and pollution prevention and product stewardship.
  • Conserve and protect biodiversity in and around of our operational sites in cooperation with relevant stakeholders.
  • Achieve No-Net Loss at our project operations by implementing the mitigation hierarchy by avoiding, minimizing, and restoring the direct impacts and offsetting the residual impacts.
  • Reduce our impact on environment and climate change and regularly assess environmental risks and opportunities associated with our projects by taking appropriate mitigation & adaptation measures.
  • Participate in green building certification programs and advocate for the utilization of environmentally sustainable materials and technologies in construction.
  • Create environmental awareness through continuous engagement with internal and external stakeholders to adopt principles and practices in alignment with this policy and industry best practices.
  • Monitor, measure, and report the progress on environmental performance in compliance with national and internationally recognized protocols and communicate approach and achievements to relevant stakeholders.
  • Undertake internal and third-party audits at regular intervals to assess the environmental performance and subsequent mitigative actions.

Commitment to Sustainable Procurement

In its efforts to reduce the effects of climate change, NeoLiv will strive to source and utilize products that are:

  • Locally sourced or recovered materials.
  • Materials with low embodied carbon.
  • Low-emission VOC materials.
  • Easily recyclable materials and packaging.
  • Materials that provide information on environmental impacts.
  • Materials that reveal potential health risks.
  • Rapidly renewable materials and those containing recycled content.

Violation or Breach of this Policy:

Any violation or breach of this policy shall be dealt with procedures framed by the company from time to time.
