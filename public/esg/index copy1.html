Our Vision

Our vision is to become India's leading real estate platform through sustainability, quality and service excellence.

Our ESG strategy is built on three core pillars that drive NeoLiv's long-term vision for sustainable, inclusive, and responsible growth.

Our Pillars

Our ESG strategy and strategic pillars are driven by clearly defined focus areas, mapped to Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) with short-, mid-, and long-term goals. These are aligned with national and global benchmarks to ensure we contribute meaningfully to sustainable development.

We believe in transparency and accountability—tracking, reviewing, and disclosing our progress annually. Through our ESG disclosures we aim to communicate our work and consideration to our stakeholders, thereby delivering long-term value to our investors, partners, customers, and communities.

Sustainable Living Spaces at NeoLiv reflect our commitment to building thoughtfully designed homes that are energy-efficient, resource conscious, and environmentally responsible.

Our Promises

Achieve 50% renewable energy usage across clubhouses and common areas

By harnessing solar and other renewable sources, we're significantly reducing dependency on conventional energy.

Ensure 100% of streetlights are powered by solar energy

Creating energy-efficient, self-sustaining communities with clean energy solutions.

Ensure 100% alignment with Air Quality Index standards

Maintaining clean indoor air for healthier, more breathable environments for all residents.

Use 100% recycled water for maintenance and landscaping

Conserving fresh water resources while maintaining lush green spaces.

Implement Rainwater Harvesting Systems at all locations

Capturing rainwater helps recharge groundwater and supports water conservation, especially during dry seasons.

Ensure Zero Waste to Landfill across all construction sites

Through segregation, recycling, and responsible disposal, we’re minimizing waste generation, thereby reducing landfill use.

Install organic waste composters at all sites

Composting food and garden waste into nutrient-rich compost reduces methane emissions and supports on-site landscaping.

Achieve ISO 14001 Environmental Certification for every site

This globally recognized certification helps us manage environmental responsibilities and enhance sustainability performance.

100% plantation of native species at all sites

Supporting local biodiversity with native plants that thrive naturally and require minimal water and maintenance.
